14 ways of Father helps a mother
  • 1. Staying by during breast-feeding:

    Breastfeeding the baby in the first few weeks is very difficult for the mother. At this time she may be busy breastfeeding most of the day. As a result, he cannot do many things by himself at this time. As a partner, you should keep him in the most comfortable position during this time. During this time, you can bring him water or juice along with some healthy snacks. Every now and then you can ask if he needs anything or if he needs to do some housework

  • Keeping the child in the lap:

    Apart from breastfeeding, there is nothing that the father of a newborn cannot do. Swaddling is a great opportunity for fathers to bond with their babies. This skin-to-skin connection, also known as 'kangaroo care', is not only for mother and baby, but you, the father, feel free to spend as much time as you want. This time will also be a little rest for the new mother.

  • Let the mother sleep

    New mothers need more sleep. If the father has helped the mother in all the work after the birth of the child, then both the mother and the father need enough sleep. It is usually very difficult for a new mother to get enough sleep because she has to spend most of the sleepless nights with the baby. During this time father can give a mother enough sleep. You can spend some alone time with the baby by allowing the mother to sleep. Reassure the mother that you will attend to the baby properly.

  • Change the baby's diaper:

    You can do some of the baby chores yourself during the time you stay at home. Newborn diapers need to be changed several times a day. Father should do this whenever he gets a chance. Doing it once or twice will make the job much easier and the baby's mother will be very happy to have you as her partner.

  • Bathe the child:

    Bathing a newborn may seem like a daunting task, but it's actually a bonding activity. For the first few days you can follow the baby's mother and master how to do the task. If mom can do it then surely you can too. Bathing a baby is a very fun moment

  • Get up at night to feed the baby:

    Whether the baby is breastfed or formula fed, you as the father can sometimes take over the responsibility of feeding the baby. After the baby breastfeeds, ask the mother to pump enough milk for the night. And eat the formula yourself according to the quantity. Remember that if you take over some nights, it will be a huge opportunity for the new mom to rest

  • Cook for the baby's mother

    Since a large part of the Mother's Day is spent on childcare, it is difficult for her to find time for other activities. If your partner used to do the cooking before the arrival of the child in the family, then it may not be fair to expect the same to happen after the arrival of the child. Instead, why not surprise the new mom by doing the cooking for her during this difficult time.
