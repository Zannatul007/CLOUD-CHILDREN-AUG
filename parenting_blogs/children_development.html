Learn about children development and make better future
A good relationship between the child and the parents helps in building a bright future for the child. You can build a beautiful relationship with the child by giving the child various small tasks, rhymes, stories and giving time alone. As a conscious parent, you too can help develop good behavior, positive personality, and morals and values in your child. An online Early Childhood Development course is being organized by our organization Teachers Time for the parents of Bangladesh. If you are a parent of a child aged 1-8 years, this course will be very useful for you. From this two-month course, you will learn-
  • Techniques for creating a good relationship with the child
  • Stages of baby brain development
  • What you can do for the proper mental development of the child
  • Child's learning style and teaching strategies accordingly
  • Teaching through sports etc.
  • Can bring behavioral changes in the child
