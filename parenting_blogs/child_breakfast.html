Child Breakfast

Mothers wake up every morning and panic about their child's breakfast or food. And if the child's age is between one and five, then there is no word. Because it is at this age that mothers have to suffer the most with baby food. Although it is not possible to completely eliminate this thought, it can be reduced to some extent by adopting some methods. Remember that it is very important for children or adults to eat a full meal in the morning. It is very important to eat a full stomach in the morning for the health of the whole day. So the best way is to feed the baby khichuri in the morning.

Khichuri

soft cooked khichuri can easily be eaten by children for breakfast.

Oats

You can give bananas or eggs with oats. Oats should be soaked in milk for 2/3 minutes after it becomes soft and served. Instead of using sugar for small children, you can cook with honey or date powder.

Milk-Bread

Bread soaked in milk can be fed to children. It is easily digested and liked by children.

Sujeer Halwa

Homemade semolina halwa is a great food for toddlers.

Fruit Purees

Various fruit purees are a nutritious food for babies. But you can give it directly in the morning or on an empty stomach with other food without any fruit.

Pasta or Noodles

You can give your child pasta or noodles for breakfast. But never use the extra spices or tasting salt in their packet. You can break the egg into the noodles along with some vegetables.

Dates, almonds and others

Dates, almonds and other dry fruits are very beneficial for children. So try to feed these foods to your child regularly.

Apart from these, you can give your child various types of food. Try to have any fruit or fruit juice with breakfast. Always feed your baby home-cooked meals.
